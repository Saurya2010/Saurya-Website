How a project runs

From work order to final delivery

PHASE 1

Brief & Script

Concept note and script developed in direct consultation with the client, with recce visits where multi-location shooting is required.

PHASE 2

Production

In-house crews shoot on 4K-capable equipment, coordinating directly with institutional stakeholders for interviews and access.

PHASE 3

Post-Production

Editing, colour grading, animation, voice-over and subtitling — all completed by in-house teams, not outsourced.

PHASE 4

Review & Handover

Draft cuts submitted for approval, revisions incorporated, and final assets delivered with all raw footage and source files.
