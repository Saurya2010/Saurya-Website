Who We Are

The Pioneer in e-Academia & Digital Learning

Saurya Edunext Gen (P) Ltd. is a leading e-Learning company dedicated to providing customised services in e-academia and digital education as per the needs of individual learners and institutions alike.

Managed by a team of IT and management professionals with a network of over 500 resource persons — Academicians, Scientists, Technology Experts and Trainers — each with 10–15 years of experience in e-Learning, e-Content, Digital Literacy, Vocational Training and Video Conferencing.

Our services have been adopted by INFLIBNET, UGC, SPA and Central Universities under the NMEICT initiative of MHRD, Government of India.

◈ Vision

To be India's foremost digital education company — empowering every learner through innovative, accessible and outcome-focused e-Learning solutions.

◈ Mission

To deliver customised, technology-driven educational content and training solutions that bridge the skill gap and transform learning for institutions and professionals.
